Gain a 360-degree view of consumer behavior

Access simple dashboards for a one-click analysis of massive datasets (spending, foot traffic, and survey panels) to see where, how, and why consumers spend their time and money – with your business AND with your competitors.

Add a qualitative lens to quantitative data using video surveys, and uncover trends with AI-powered analysis tools. Or launch a DIY survey for a quick and easy way to gather insights from customers and consumers.

Benchmark against competitors and capture more market share

Analyze consumer spending and foot traffic behavior, and deploy targeted surveys to consumers who just visited a store or a website. Gain a holistic view of the market by comparing your performance against competitors with unblinded data. Don’t wait for independent reports on competitors – with Medallia you can quickly see which competitors are capturing share and specific opportunities for improvement.

The combination of real-time, unblinded behavioral data and survey data gives your team unprecedented insight into your customers and competitors, so you can get ahead of market needs and empower your team to act.

Drive rich, authentic qualitative insights with video feedback at scale

Feedback comes to life with video. Capture, analyze and share consumer feedback via video in a single platform. Automatically extract meaningful insights so you can tell powerful stories that inspire better actions to deliver the best customer experiences.

Design, launch, and analyze your own DIY surveys

Create, edit, and distribute surveys in no time with Medallia’s easy-to-use survey builder. Choose a predesigned template, based on decades of market research expertise, or start from scratch.

Leverage real-time reporting to spot trends earlier, determine response rates more accurately, send reminders to increase responses, and easily share results across your organization.
